Jimmy G Tee

The Jimmy G Tee is an adjustable kicking tee that has been used by some of the worlds best goal kickers including Andrew Mehrtens, Simon Culhane, John Earls, Dan Carter, Gavin Henson & Jimmy Gopperth to name a few.

Previously known as the Simpkin Tee, it was designed by the late rugby legend George Simpkin and was one of the first kicking tees made. Simon Culhane still holds the record for the most successful kicks in a Rugby World Cup match with 20 kicks (using this tee). Jimmy Gopperth, the 3x English Premiership Golden Boot Winner has scored over 3600 points off this tee.
